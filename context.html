1990s Cultural Anxieties

Media Spectacle

The O.J. Simpson trial (1994) mirrors the film’s themes of performative violence, blurred reality, and sensationalism. The trial became a media circus—reality turned into entertainment, a hallmark of postmodern anxiety over truth and image.

Consumerism Critique

The "$5 milkshake" scene satirizes 1990s excess and brand obsession. It mocks the emptiness behind luxury goods—part of a postmodern critique of surface over substance, where identity is performed through consumption.
